Hello, My Name is Clinton.

30 years young, born and raised in State College, Pennsylvania. Highly motivated web developer with international client portfolio.

I currently work at Agrian, writing services to shuffle massive amounts of agricultural data and make farmers more productive.

I'm a frequent open source contributor, a robotics instructor and father of two (who I have also taught to code!) I love the practice of software craftsmanship, and I strive to amaze every client I work with. Let's build stuff together!
